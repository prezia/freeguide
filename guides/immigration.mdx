## Express Entry — how it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check eligibility">
    Federal Skilled Worker, Canadian Experience Class, or Federal Skilled Trades.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your profile">
    Submit your profile to the pool. You'll receive a CRS score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get invited">
    IRCC draws candidates regularly. Top scores receive an Invitation to Apply (ITA).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your PR application">
    Within 60 days of receiving the ITA.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Improving your CRS score

- Improve your language test results (IELTS, CELPIP, TEF)
- Get a Canadian job offer (LMIA-backed)
- Pursue a provincial nomination (\+600 points)
- Add French language results
- Higher education credentials

<Warning>
  Immigration rules change often. Always verify on the official IRCC website before applying.
</Warning>
