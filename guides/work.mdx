## Work permits

Most foreigners need a work permit. There are two main types:

- **Employer-specific permit** — tied to one employer, usually requires an LMIA.
- **Open work permit** — work for almost any employer (e.g., PGWP, spouse permits).

## How to find a job

<Steps>
  <Step title="Adapt your resume">
    Canadian-style: 1–2 pages, no photo, no personal info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the right platforms">
    Job Bank, LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Network">
    LinkedIn outreach and local meetups are powerful in Canada.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interview prep">
    Practice behavioral (STAR method) and technical questions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  A Canadian-recognized credential evaluation (WES, ICAS) makes your foreign diplomas comparable for employers and immigration.
</Tip>
