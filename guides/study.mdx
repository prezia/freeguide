## Why study in Canada?

Canada is one of the top destinations for international students — world-class universities, affordable tuition (compared to the US/UK), and post-graduation work permits.

## Student visa basics

To study in Canada for more than 6 months, you need a **Study Permit**.

**Requirements:**

- Letter of acceptance from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI)
- Proof of funds (tuition \+ living expenses)
- Valid passport
- Clean criminal record
- Medical exam (if required)

<Note>
  Processing times vary by country. Apply at least 3–6 months before your program starts.
</Note>

## After graduation

Most programs make you eligible for a **Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP)** — work in Canada for up to 3 years and build a path to permanent residency.
